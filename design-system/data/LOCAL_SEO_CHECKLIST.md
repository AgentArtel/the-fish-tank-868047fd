# Local SEO — Google Business Profile & Citations Checklist

**The Fish Tank · 8371 700 W, Sandy, UT 84070 · (801) 887-7000**

The off-site half of local authority. The website (catalog + content engine) earns topical
authority; **this** is what wins the Google **Map Pack** and "near me" searches across the Salt
Lake Valley. Work it top to bottom; revisit quarterly.

> **THE GOLDEN RULE — NAP consistency.** Name, Address, Phone must be **byte-identical**
> everywhere (site, GBP, every directory). Pick one canonical format and never deviate:
> - **Name:** `The Fish Tank`  (no tagline, no city suffix)
> - **Address:** `8371 700 W, Sandy, UT 84070`
> - **Phone:** `(801) 887-7000`
> - **Website:** `https://thefishtank.com`
> Inconsistent NAP is the #1 thing that suppresses local rankings. Source it on the site from
> `site_settings` / `store_locations` so it can't drift.

---

## 1. Google Business Profile — claim & verify
- [ ] Claim/create the profile at business.google.com; **verify** (postcard, phone, or video).
- [ ] Primary category: **`Tropical fish store`** (or `Aquarium shop` / `Pet store` if unavailable).
- [ ] Additional categories: `Pet supply store`, `Aquarium`, `Store` — only ones that truly apply.
- [ ] Confirm exact NAP matches the golden rule above.
- [ ] Service area: list Sandy + Salt Lake Valley cities (Salt Lake City, Draper, South Jordan,
      West Jordan, Midvale, Lehi, Murray, Cottonwood Heights) if you deliver/serve them.

## 2. Profile completeness (Google favors 100% complete)
- [ ] **Hours** — exactly match the site: Mon–Fri 11:30a–8p, Sat 11a–6p, Sun 11a–4p. Set special
      holiday hours as they come up.
- [ ] **Description** (750 chars) — lead with what + where: "Utah's saltwater fish & coral store
      in Sandy…", mention WYSIWYG corals, livestock guarantee, in-store reef expertise.
- [ ] **Website link** → homepage; add deep links for Products if available.
- [ ] **Attributes** — in-store shopping, in-store pickup, wheelchair accessible, payments, etc.
- [ ] **Opening date**, **logo** (the blue-tang mark), and **cover photo**.
- [ ] **Products** — add key categories/items (corals, fish, inverts, dry goods) with photos +
      prices; this can mirror the catalog's featured items.
- [ ] **Services** — water testing, tank setup/maintenance, special orders, frag swaps (if offered).

## 3. Photos & video (huge for conversion + ranking)
- [ ] Logo + cover. Then **20–30+ real photos**: storefront/exterior (helps Google match the
      location), interior aisles, display tanks, livestock close-ups, team at work.
- [ ] Geotag-fresh, well-lit, no heavy filters. Add new photos **monthly** (signals an active business).
- [ ] A short walkthrough video of the showroom.
- [ ] Reuse the site's `public-media` shots so branding is consistent.

## 4. Reviews — the strongest local ranking + trust factor
- [ ] Create a short review link (GBP "Get more reviews") → QR code at the register + on receipts.
- [ ] Ask every happy customer at checkout; train staff on the one-line ask.
- [ ] **Respond to every review** (positive and negative) within ~48h, professionally.
- [ ] Target a steady drip (e.g. a few per week) rather than spikes.
- [ ] **Do NOT** put `Review`/`AggregateRating` JSON-LD on your own site for these — Google
      penalizes self-serving markup. Reviews live on GBP; the site can *link* to them.

## 5. Google Posts & Q&A (keep the profile active)
- [ ] Post weekly: new arrivals, weekend specials, frag swaps, livestock highlights, events.
- [ ] Seed the **Q&A** with real FAQs (hours, livestock guarantee, do you ship, parking) and
      answer them yourself; monitor for customer questions.
- [ ] Enable **messaging** if you can respond quickly.

## 6. NAP citations — build & clean (consistency > volume)
Create/claim and align NAP on these. Mark each ✅ once it matches the golden rule exactly.

**Tier 1 — core aggregators & majors**
- [ ] Apple Maps (Apple Business Connect)
- [ ] Bing Places
- [ ] Facebook Page (+ Instagram linked)
- [ ] Yelp
- [ ] Data aggregators: Data Axle, Localeze/Neustar, Foursquare (feed many downstream directories)

**Tier 2 — general directories**
- [ ] Yellow Pages (YP.com), MapQuest, Nextdoor (claim the local business), Better Business Bureau,
      Chamber of Commerce (Sandy / Salt Lake), Manta, Hotfrog, Cylex, Brownbook.

**Tier 3 — niche / industry & local**
- [ ] Aquarium-hobby directories & forums (local reef club listings, Reef2Reef vendor area if eligible).
- [ ] Local Utah business directories, Visit Sandy / city listings, local news business pages.
- [ ] Suppliers' "where to buy" / dealer-locator pages (lighting, salt, equipment brands you carry).

**Hygiene**
- [ ] Search the business name + phone; **find and fix or remove duplicate listings** (duplicates
      split ranking signals).
- [ ] Fix any old address/phone from a previous location everywhere it appears.

## 7. On-site signals that support the profile (cross-check vs the build)
- [ ] `LocalBusiness`/`PetStore` JSON-LD with matching NAP, geo, hours (already in the kit's
      `index.html`; wire it from `store_locations`).
- [ ] A real **Visit Us / Contact** page: embedded Google Map, address, hours, phone (click-to-call),
      parking notes, photos.
- [ ] Footer NAP on every page (from `site_settings`).
- [ ] Embedded Google Map of the actual listing on the contact page.
- [ ] Local landing/content (guides referencing Utah water, Salt Lake Valley reefing) — `articles`.

## 8. Local backlinks (authority beyond citations)
- [ ] Local reef/aquarium clubs (sponsor, host a meet, get listed/linked).
- [ ] Local press / community blogs (store opening, events, expansions).
- [ ] Partnerships: schools, maintenance clients, complementary local businesses.
- [ ] Brand "dealer locator" links from products you stock.
- [ ] Event listings (frag swaps) on community calendars (also feeds `events` + `Event` JSON-LD).

## 9. Measure & maintain (monthly)
- [ ] GBP **Performance** (calls, direction requests, searches, photo views).
- [ ] Google Search Console (impressions/clicks for local + product/guide queries; fix coverage).
- [ ] Track Map-Pack rank for core terms: "saltwater fish store near me", "coral shop Salt Lake
      City", "reef store Utah", "aquarium store Sandy UT".
- [ ] Review velocity + average rating; respond to all.
- [ ] Add fresh photos + a Google Post; publish/refresh at least one guide or post.
- [ ] Re-audit NAP consistency across citations.

---

### Priority order if time is short
1. Claim + verify GBP, perfect NAP, set categories & hours.
2. Photos (storefront + tanks) and the review link/QR + ask-at-checkout habit.
3. Tier-1 citations (Apple, Bing, Facebook, Yelp, aggregators) with exact NAP.
4. Weekly Google Posts + answer Q&A.
5. On-site: Visit Us page + JSON-LD + footer NAP.
6. Then Tier-2/3 citations and local backlinks over time.
